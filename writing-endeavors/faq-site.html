FAQ Page

This page will be deticated towards helping everyone understand writing and it's importance in our daily lives.

Question -

Why is writing important?

Answer -

Writing makes up almost everything that we do as a human species. It is the way that we can communicate and talk with each other. Without it, our ideas, thoughts, and imaginations would be void.

Question -

How can I get into writing myself?

Answer -

A way that everyone can get into writing is through an event and website called NaNoWriMo. It is a website with many resources and simple ways that everyone can get into writing themselves. Checking them out would help even those people that are wanting to improve their craft.
