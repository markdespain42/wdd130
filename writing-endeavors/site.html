Purpose of the site

This site is more than just a purpose. It is to encourage people to read or write. Literature builds our entire lives. Without it we would be nothing. All of our ideas are put on paper or electronics for a reason. We need more people to move away from social media and embrace more wholesome and productive ways to live life. Social Media is useful, but not to waste time and escape reality. Writing and reading is all about escaping reality and making it your own. The beauty of writing and reading is only bound by your imagination.
